# Data Deletion Policy

Users can request deletion of their data by contacting us at:

auratics.studio@gmail.com

Since this app does not require user accounts and does not store personal data on servers, most data is stored locally on the user’s device.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically deleted from your device.

For advertising-related data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob), users may manage or reset their advertising ID in their device settings.
